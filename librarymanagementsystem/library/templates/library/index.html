Welcome To Easy Library

Our platform is desigend with simplicity and functionality in mind, making it easy for students to explore and borrow books, while giving administrators the tools to efficiently manage resources. By bridging the gap between students and the wealth of knowledge within our library, we aim to nurture the next generation of thinkers, creators and leaders.





About Us

We are dedicated to fostering a culture of learning and discovery within our school community. Our library management system provides students with seamless access to a wide range of educational materials, from text books, to fiction, ensuring that learning never stops, both inside and outside the classroom. We strive to create an environment where curiosity is encouraged, and students have the tools they need to excel academically.

Student Borrowing and Return

Admin Resource Management

Administrators have full control over the library's collection, from adding new titles to aproving and issuing books.

Students can keep track of a list of books they have borrowed and the time left to return them, as well as the accumulated fine if the deadline has expired.
